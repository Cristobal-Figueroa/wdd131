Mountain Trail Explorers - Site Plan

Your Guide to Mountain Adventures

Site Name

Mountain Trail Explorers

This name was chosen to represent a community of hiking enthusiasts who explore mountain trails. The name clearly communicates the focus on mountain hiking and exploration, making it immediately relevant to the target audience of outdoor enthusiasts.

Site Purpose

The Mountain Trail Explorers website serves as a comprehensive resource for mountain hiking enthusiasts. The site aims to:

  • Provide detailed information about various mountain trails, including difficulty levels, elevation gains, and estimated completion times
  • Offer safety tips, recommended gear for different types of trails, and seasonal hiking recommendations
  • Create a community platform where hikers can share their experiences and connect with fellow enthusiasts
  • Promote responsible hiking practices and environmental conservation

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "I'm planning a hiking trip this weekend. What trails are suitable for intermediate hikers in the local mountain range?"

Scenario 2: "What essential gear should I pack for a day hike in mountainous terrain during spring?"

Scenario 3: "How can I connect with other hikers who are interested in group expeditions to challenging mountain trails?"

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E5A88 (Mountain Blue) - Used for headers, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons

Secondary Color: #8DAA54 (Forest Green) - Used for secondary headings and highlighted information

Accent Color: #E8C547 (Sunshine Yellow) - Used for important alerts, highlights, and interactive elements

Background Color: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) - Used for the main background to ensure readability

Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray) - Used for main text content for optimal readability

Typography

Primary Font: "Montserrat" - Used for all headings and navigation elements to convey a modern, clean aesthetic that reflects the outdoor adventure theme

Secondary Font: "Open Sans" - Used for body text and content for optimal readability across devices

Accent Font: "Pacifico" - Used sparingly for special section titles to add visual interest
